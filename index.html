Our Story

Three generations.
One standard.

Alliance Glass Pack is a dynamic packaging solutions provider and a subsidiary of Chaudhary Glass — a well-established name in premium packaging with decades of industry experience.

Now led by the third generation of the Chaudhary family, we combine inherited expertise with a modern, forward-looking approach. Our South Africa operations, headquartered in Cape Town, are backed by a global network spanning five warehouses across two continents.

We don't just supply glass. We partner with brands to create packaging that tells their story — from concept to shelf.
